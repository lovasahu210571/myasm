Welcome

Celebrating Tradition, Fostering Unity, Since 1985

Established in 1985 by a group of like-minded and dedicated individuals, Astaprahar Sankeertan Mandal has been a beacon of unity and culture for the Utkal Bandhu community residing in NCH Colony, Kanjurmarg (West) and beyond. Our journey began with a simple yet profound mission: to bring together the sons and daughters of Odisha, irrespective of their background, to create a common platform for the promotion and popularization of the rich culture and heritage of our beloved state.

Our Vision and Values

At Astaprahar Sankeertan Mandal, we hold dear the essence of our cultural roots, fostering a sense of belonging and camaraderie among our fellow Utkal Bandhus. Our commitment to preserving and propagating the cultural heritage of Odisha is unwavering. We believe that culture is a binding force that transcends boundaries and brings people together.

A Non-Profit Endeavor

We are proud to be a non-profit organization, driven solely by the passion and commitment of our members. This means that every event, every program, and every initiative we undertake is motivated by a genuine love for our culture and a desire to strengthen the bonds within our community. We stand as a testament to the power of unity and the beauty of cultural diversity.

Join Us in Our Celebration of Culture

As we invite you to explore our website, we hope you'll join us in our celebration of the vibrant culture and heritage of Odisha. Discover the latest updates on our upcoming events, explore the rich tapestry of Odia art and traditions, and connect with like-minded individuals who share your love for our culture. Astaprahar Sankeertan Mandal is not just an organization; it's a home where you can embrace your roots, find new friends, and contribute to the preservation and propagation of our cultural heritage. We are a family, and we welcome you with open arms to be a part of this wonderful journey.

About Us

Our Founders

Astaprahar Sankeertan Mandal, a proud symbol of cultural preservation and unity, was born in the year 1985 through the vision and dedication of a group of remarkable individuals. Shree Vijay Padhi, Shree Padma Charan Sahu, Late Shree Lingaraj Sahu, Shree Chenaya Naidu, Shree Rohit Padhy, and many other like-minded souls united their efforts to create a platform that would become a testament to the unwavering spirit of the Utkal Bandhu community.

A Journey of Heritage and Unity

Since its inception, Astaprahar Sankeertan Mandal has been at the forefront of promoting and popularizing the rich culture and heritage of Odisha. Our journey is a story of culture and community, where the roots of Odisha are celebrated, and the ties among Utkal Bandhus are strengthened.
