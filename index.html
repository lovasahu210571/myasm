Welcome Page

In the year 1985 a group of like minded and dedicated people joined together to bring all Utkal Bandhu living in NCH Colony ,Kanjurmarg(west) on a common platform and to promote and popularize the rich culture and heritage of Odisha.Thus Astaprahar Sankeertan Mandal has come into existence.
